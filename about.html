About TeleHealth Global

Connecting patients to world-class care, no matter where they are.

Our Mission

TeleHealth Global is dedicated to making healthcare accessible, fast, and affordable through technology. With a growing network of verified medical professionals across every continent, we aim to break geographical barriers in health access and ensure everyone can talk to a doctor—anytime, anywhere.

Why Choose Us?

  • 🌍 Global reach with doctors speaking over 25 languages
  • 🩺 Verified professionals: doctors, therapists, specialists
  • 📹 Secure HD video consultations
  • 💳 Affordable prices, transparent fees
  • 📅 24/7 appointment availability

Meet the Founder

Daniel Maina – CEO & Founder

Daniel Maina is a visionary leader in digital health, committed to using technology to solve global healthcare inequality. With years of experience in tech and innovation, he founded TeleHealth Global to offer inclusive, international medical access to everyone—from cities to rural communities.
